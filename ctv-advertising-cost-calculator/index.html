How much does CTV / streaming TV advertising cost?

Connected TV is bought on CPM, and 2026 rates run about $25–$65 depending on platform and targeting. Premium streaming (Hulu, Disney+) sits around $30–$50, while ad-supported free tiers like Tubi and Pluto are cheaper at $15–$30. Unlike traditional TV, CTV lets you target by household, ZIP and interest — great for local service businesses.

Is CTV worth it for local businesses?

CTV pairs TV's trust and sight-sound-motion with digital targeting and tracking. Geo-targeted or addressable CTV can zero in on your service area, so even a modest local budget reaches the right households. Direct response is modest per impression, so measure by cost per lead and view-through conversions, not clicks.

Frequently asked questions

How much does connected TV advertising cost?
Around $25–$65 CPM in 2026. Premium streaming is higher; ad-supported free tiers are cheaper. Minimum budgets are far lower than traditional TV.
Is CTV cheaper than cable TV?
CTV usually has a higher CPM than broadcast, but far better targeting and no minimum-market buys, so wasted spend is lower for a local advertiser.
Can I target my local area on CTV?
Yes. Geo-targeted and addressable CTV can serve ads by ZIP, DMA or household, which is ideal for service-area businesses.
