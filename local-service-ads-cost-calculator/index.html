How much do Google Local Services Ads cost?

LSAs are billed per lead, not per click. The national home-services average is about $53 per lead, but it varies widely by trade and market — competitive metros run $90+ while smaller markets can be $30 or less. Only charged for genuine leads (a call or message), and you can dispute invalid ones.

Why LSAs beat regular Google Ads for the trades

You pay only for leads (not clicks), you get the green Google Guaranteed badge that builds trust, and your listing sits at the very top of local results. After booking rate and closing, LSA cost per booked job is usually far lower than traditional PPC, which runs $80–$200 per lead.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a Google LSA lead cost?
Around $53 on average for home services in 2026; roughly $40–$90 for plumbing, $60–$120 for HVAC, $30–$75 for electrical.
Can I dispute bad LSA leads?
Yes. Google lets you dispute leads that are spam, wrong service, or out of area, and credits valid disputes so you're not charged.
Are LSAs better than Google Ads?
For local trades, usually yes — pay-per-lead pricing and the Google Guaranteed badge typically deliver a lower cost per booked job than click-based PPC.
