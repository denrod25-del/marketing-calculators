How much does podcast advertising cost?

Podcast ads are priced per 1,000 downloads (CPM). In 2026, programmatic and pre-produced ads run ~$18–$30 CPM, while host-read mid-roll spots run ~$25–$50 — the host's personal endorsement is why they cost (and convert) more. Niche business and finance shows can command $40+.

Why host-read ads convert

Listeners trust the host, so a personal endorsement carries far more weight than a produced spot. Give the host a unique promo code or vanity URL so you can track downloads-to-conversions. Cost = (downloads ÷ 1,000) × CPM × number of episodes.

Frequently asked questions

How much does a podcast ad cost?
Usually $18–$30 CPM for produced/programmatic and $25–$50 for host-read. A 20,000-download show at a $30 CPM is about $600 per episode.
Are host-read ads worth the premium?
Often yes — the host's endorsement drives higher trust and response, which usually offsets the higher CPM.
How do I track podcast ad ROI?
Use a unique promo code or vanity URL per show so conversions can be attributed to the campaign.
