How much do Nextdoor ads cost?

Nextdoor's self-serve Ads Manager runs an auction. Recommended minimums are roughly $2 CPC and $10 CPM, with real-world local CPCs often landing around $2.50–$3.50 and CPMs around $10–$20. Local Deals can start much smaller (a few dollars). Because Nextdoor is neighborhood-verified, recommendations there convert a lot like referrals.

Why Nextdoor works for local services

Nextdoor users are verified neighbors actively asking "who's a good plumber/roofer/electrician?" — high-intent, hyper-local, and trust-driven. Ads plus an active free Business Page (responding to recommendations) compound: paid reach seeds the organic word-of-mouth the platform is built on.

Frequently asked questions

How much does it cost to advertise on Nextdoor?
Roughly $10–$20 CPM or $2.50–$3.50 per click via Ads Manager; Local Deals can start at just a few dollars.
Are Nextdoor ads worth it?
For local home and service businesses, often yes — the audience is verified neighbors with high local intent, so recommendations convert like referrals.
Do I need a paid ad to use Nextdoor?
No — a free Business Page and responding to neighbor recommendations drives organic leads; ads amplify that reach.
