How much do Angi and Thumbtack leads cost?

Home-services marketplaces charge per lead, typically $15–$120+ per homeowner contact depending on job type and market. The catch: most leads are shared with 3–8 competing pros, so your win rate is lower than an exclusive lead — which is why the booking rate is the number that really drives your cost per job.

Shared vs exclusive leads

Because a shared lead goes to several contractors, speed matters enormously — the first to respond usually wins. Model your realistic booking rate (often 10–25% on shared platforms) rather than assuming you close every lead. Exclusive leads cost more but convert far better.

Frequently asked questions

How much does an Angi or Thumbtack lead cost?
Usually $15–$120+ per lead depending on trade, job size and market. Larger jobs and competitive metros cost more.
Are marketplace leads worth it?
They can be if you respond fast and your cost per booked job beats other channels. Because leads are shared, speed-to-lead is the difference between profit and loss.
How do I lower my cost per job?
Respond within minutes, qualify quickly, and dispute invalid leads. A higher booking rate cuts your effective cost per job more than a lower cost per lead.
