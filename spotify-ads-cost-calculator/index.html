How much do Spotify ads cost?

Spotify runs on a CPM model: audio ads typically cost $15–$25 per 1,000 impressions and video ads $20–$35. The self-serve Ad Studio has a $250 minimum, which reaches roughly 10,000–16,000 listeners. You can target by age, location, genre, playlist and even real-world context like workouts.

Making audio ads work

Listeners can't click a running audio ad, so give a memorable call to action — a short URL, a promo code, or "search [brand]." Keep it to one message, use a real voice, and geo-target your service area. Pair audio with a companion display card for the moment listeners glance at their phone.
