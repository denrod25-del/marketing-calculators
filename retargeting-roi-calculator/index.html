Why retargeting has outsized ROI

Retargeting (remarketing) shows ads only to people who already visited your site or engaged with you. They know you, so they click and convert at much higher rates than cold audiences — often several times higher — while the CPM stays low (around $3–$8). That combination makes it consistently one of the best-ROI channels available.

How it works

A pixel drops a cookie on visitors; your ads then follow them across the web, social and video. You're spending only to re-reach warm prospects, so wasted impressions are minimal. Cap frequency so you stay present without being annoying.

Getting the most from it

Segment by behavior — cart/quote abandoners convert best, so bid more there. Match the message to where they left off, use a clear offer, and set frequency caps and a burn window so you stop paying to chase people who already bought.

Frequently asked questions

How much does retargeting cost?
CPMs are low, roughly $3–$8, because you're re-reaching a small warm audience. Total spend depends on your visitor volume.
Why does retargeting convert better?
It only reaches people who already know you, so click-through and conversion rates run well above cold prospecting.
Is retargeting worth it?
Usually yes — it's often the highest-ROI paid channel because you spend only on warm prospects. Cap frequency to avoid fatigue.
