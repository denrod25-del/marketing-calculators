What is quota attainment?

Quota attainment is how much of your sales target you've achieved: actual ÷ quota × 100. It's the headline measure of sales performance for reps and teams, and the basis for most commission and forecasting decisions.

Attainment vs pace

Raw attainment doesn't tell you if you're on track — pace does. If you're 75% to quota but 80% through the period, you're slightly behind. This tool compares attainment to the share of the period elapsed and projects where you'll land if the current run rate holds.

Using it

Check pace regularly, not just at period end, so there's time to react. If projected finish is below target, you need more pipeline or a higher close rate now. Pair this with pipeline coverage and sales velocity to see whether the gap is a volume problem or a conversion problem.

Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate quota attainment?
Divide actual sales by your quota and multiply by 100. $375,000 against a $500,000 quota is 75% attainment.
How do I know if I'm on pace?
Compare attainment to how much of the period has elapsed. Ahead of the elapsed percentage means you're on pace; behind means you need to catch up.
What is projected finish?
Your current run rate extended to the end of the period — attainment divided by the share of time elapsed.
