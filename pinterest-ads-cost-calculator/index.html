How much do Pinterest ads cost?

Pinterest is among the cheapest paid-social platforms in 2026: about $2–$5 CPM, $0.50–$1.50 per click, and a $7–$8 cost per acquisition for retail — the lowest CPA of the major networks. Home, decor, DIY, food and fashion perform especially well because users are actively planning purchases.

What works on Pinterest

Pinterest is a visual search engine, so treat pins like SEO: bright vertical images, keyword-rich titles and descriptions, and links to a fast, relevant landing page. Users are in planning mode with high purchase intent, which is why costs stay low and conversion holds up — ideal for home and lifestyle offers.
