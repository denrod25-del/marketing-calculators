Call Tracking & Lead Attribution

If you can't tell which ad made the phone ring, you're guessing. Here's how to know.

The local attribution problem

Most local leads come by phone, and most businesses can't say which marketing produced each call. Without that, you're flying blind — pouring money into channels you hope work and cutting ones that might be your best. Attribution turns guesswork into decisions.

Call tracking numbers explained

A call tracking number is a unique phone number that forwards to your real line. Put a different number on each channel — one for your LSAs, one for the billboard, one for the truck wrap — and every call is automatically tagged to its source, with recordings and timestamps you can review.

Dynamic number insertion

For your website, dynamic number insertion (DNI) swaps the displayed number based on how the visitor arrived — Google Ads, organic search, a Facebook ad — without breaking your listed number for SEO. That's how you attribute website calls to the exact campaign that drove them.

Tag your digital campaigns

Add UTM tags to your ad and email links so clicks are labeled by source, medium and campaign in your analytics. Combined with call tracking, you can follow a customer from the ad they clicked to the form they filled or the call they made.

Connect calls to booked jobs

A tracked call is only useful if you know whether it became a job. Log outcomes — booked, quoted, no-show — against each call source so you can measure cost per booked job by channel, not just cost per call. That's the number that should drive your budget.

Act on what you learn

Once the data is flowing, shift budget toward the channels that produce profitable booked jobs and cut the ones that don't. Review monthly. Attribution isn't a one-time setup — it's the feedback loop that makes every future marketing dollar smarter.

Frequently asked questions

What is call tracking?
Using unique phone numbers for each marketing channel so every call is automatically tagged to the source that produced it, with recordings and timestamps.
What is dynamic number insertion?
A technique that changes the phone number shown on your website based on how the visitor arrived, so website calls can be attributed to the exact campaign.
How do I know which marketing drives the most jobs?
Combine call tracking and UTM tags with outcome logging, then measure cost per booked job by channel and shift budget toward what's profitable.
