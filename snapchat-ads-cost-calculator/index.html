How much do Snapchat ads cost?

Snapchat is one of the cheaper platforms, with CPMs generally in the $3–$8 range and a very low entry point — self-serve campaigns start around $5 per day. It skews younger, so it's strong for brands targeting Gen Z and younger millennials with vertical video and AR experiences.

Snapchat ad formats

Snap Ads (single image or video), Story Ads, Collection Ads and AR Filters/Lenses each carry slightly different CPMs. Full-screen vertical video is native to the platform; AR Lenses cost more but drive high engagement when the creative is playful.

What works on Snapchat

Design for sound-on, full-screen vertical video that grabs attention in the first second. Keep it authentic and fast, use a clear swipe-up call to action, and lean into AR if it fits your brand. Track swipe-ups through to leads with UTMs and a mobile-first landing page.
