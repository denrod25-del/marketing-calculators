How much do TikTok ads cost?

TikTok is one of the cheaper paid-social platforms, averaging around $4–$9 CPM with CPCs of roughly $0.17–$1.00. In-Feed ads are the affordable workhorse ($4–$8 CPM); premium formats like TopView ($10–$20) and Brand Takeover ($20–$50) cost far more. Most self-serve campaigns start at a modest daily minimum.

What works on TikTok

Native, authentic video crushes polished ads — film like a creator, not a brand. Spark Ads that boost your best organic posts often perform best and cheapest. Hook in the first second, keep it short, and use a clear call to action. Great for visually satisfying trades content (before/after fixes).
