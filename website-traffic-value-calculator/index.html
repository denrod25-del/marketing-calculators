What is website traffic worth?

Traffic is only worth what it converts. Value per visit = revenue ÷ visitors, where revenue is visitors × conversion rate × average sale. Knowing your value per visit tells you how much a traffic increase is actually worth — and how much you can afford to pay per click to buy more.

Why value per visit matters

If each visit is worth $0.10 and a click costs $0.08, paid traffic profits; at $0.15 a click it loses money. The same figure sets the ROI target for SEO, content and referrals. Raising conversion or average order value lifts the value of every visit you already get.

How to increase traffic value

Improve conversion (clear CTAs, faster pages, less form friction), raise average order value (bundles, upsells), and attract higher-intent visitors rather than just more of them. Better-qualified traffic converting at a higher rate beats raw volume every time.

Frequently asked questions

How do I calculate the value of website traffic?
Multiply visitors by conversion rate and average sale for revenue, then divide by visitors for value per visit.
What is a good value per visit?
It varies widely; compare it to your cost per click. If value per visit exceeds what you pay to acquire a visit, growth is profitable.
How do I increase traffic value?
Raise conversion rate and average order value, and attract higher-intent visitors rather than just more traffic.
